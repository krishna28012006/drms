Safety Guidelines

Drainage systems should be considered as confined spaces.

  1. Always wear appropriate personal protective equipment (PPE) including gloves, goggles, and safety footwear.
  2. Ensure proper ventilation when working in or near drainage systems to avoid inhaling harmful substances.
  3. Use handrails and maintain three points of contact on ladders to prevent falls.
  4. Be cautious of sharp objects and potential pollutants in the drainage area.
  5. Implement lockout/tagout procedures to ensure machinery is deactivated during maintenance.
  6. Handle chemical substances with care and follow safety data sheets for guidance.

Take these precautions seriously to ensure a safe working environment.
